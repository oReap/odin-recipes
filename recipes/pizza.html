Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is loved by so many people from young children to adults. However, it can become expensive especially when ordering form a resturaunt so we provided a budget friendly awsome Pepporoni Pizza recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F/45째C)
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 475째F (245째C).
  2. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water; let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt; make a well in the center.
  4. Pour the yeast mixture and olive oil into the well; mix until a dough forms.
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  6. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  7. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  8. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  9. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  10. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  11. Top with pepperoni, bell peppers, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  12. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  13. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.
